Device Analysis


Device analysis revolves around the drivers and physical devices plugged into the machine. Information that was extracted from the registry are: printer drivers, USB information.

USB - information that was extracted includes, type of device, Vendor, product name, revision number, serial, installed date, plugged in date and unplugged date.

For this the following registry keys were used to extract the information from SOFTWARE and SAM hive files: 

    ControlSet001\\Control\\Print\\Environments
    ControlSet001\\Enum\\USBSTOR
